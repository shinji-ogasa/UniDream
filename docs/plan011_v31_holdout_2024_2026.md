## Interpretation

The untouched 2024+ holdout is materially weaker than the 0-12 development WFO.

What survived:

- Low turnover survived: mean turnover `0.60`, max `1.73`.
- The model stayed near B&H instead of collapsing into high-turnover trading.
- Positive AlphaEx appeared in `3/9` holdout folds, including `+11.35pt`, `+8.17pt`, and `+3.64pt`.
- The aggregate AlphaEx remained positive at `+2.32pt`.

What did not survive:

- The model did not improve drawdown: MaxDDDelta was positive in every holdout fold.
- Sharpe improvement did not appear: aggregate SharpeDelta was `-0.003`.
- Recent folds `21-23` were near-neutral to small negative on AlphaEx.

Investor-safe claim:

> Plan011 v31 is a low-turnover B&H-relative neural overlay that can harvest occasional AlphaEx right-tail while usually staying near benchmark. It is not yet a drawdown-reduction model.

Do not claim `AlphaEx >= +3pt` or `MaxDDDelta <= -3pt` on untouched holdout. The current evidence supports continued research, not production-grade risk improvement.
